Lizards

Lizards are some of the coolest animals on the planet. They are also the first animals to evolve millions of years ago!

Iguana

They have many forms of defense from changing colors to detaching their tails. Most of them are very fast

Some can even climb walls!

Lizards mostly live in dry or tropical climates, they are cold-blooded meaning that they rely on an external source of heat to regulate their body temperature and metabolism.

There are over 4,000 species of lizard giving a huge variety of looks.
